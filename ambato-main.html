Visit Ambato

Enjoy the sights and discover the flavors the city has to offer.

Recommended activities to do in town:

Fiesta de las Flores y las Frutas

Visit us in February and enjoy 3 weeks of parties, events and parades celebrating the city's founding!

Try some Colada Morada!

Head over to a neighborhood called Atocha and try some of Ambato's famous Colada Moradad, a hot purple beverage made from a variety of fruits of spices.

Visit the city's botanical garden La Liria

Continue past the Colada Morada stands in Atocha to reach La Liria, the city's beautiful botanical garden which also includes the former homes of two of the city's most illustrious figures: Juan Montalvo and Juan Leon Mera.
